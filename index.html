Hansard Ontology

This site publishes the Hansard Ontology, a small OWL ontology modelling parliamentary proceedings including sessions, meetings, sitting days, agenda items, debate contributions, Members of Parliament, bills, and Hansard documents.

  • Hansard.owl — The ontology (RDF/XML)

Ontology IRI

http://www.semanticweb.org/gmasamba/ontologies/2026/4/hansard

Purpose

The ontology is designed as an educational example for ontology engineering and as a foundation for semantic modelling of parliamentary records and Hansard documents.
